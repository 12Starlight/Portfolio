A Little About Me

I am a software engineer proficient in Java, Python, Pandas, Ruby, Rails, Mongo, Express, React, Redux, Node.js, JavaScript ES6, HTML5, SCSS, RSpec, SQL, AWS, Webpack, Git, and jQuery. At seven, I got my first erector set. As I grew up I learned how to turn ideas into reality. Later as an entrepreneur, I looked for mentors in entrepreneurship and found them. As an entrepreneur, I loved solving problems and rising to challenges. I found the financial markets interesting and became a trader. As a trader I began trading with just a few hundred dollars.
